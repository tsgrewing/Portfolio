About Me

I grew up in Mt. Juliet, TN but moved after graduating high school in 2005. I went to college in Sioux Falls, SD, studying modern foreign language and philosophy. During college I started working at a local coffee roasting company, and in 2011 I took the next step in my coffee career and moved to Seattle to roast for Stumptown Coffee. In 2014 I moved to Chicago, and lived there until 2017, mostly working in the specialty coffee and craft beer industries. My wife and I moved to South Florida in early 2017. We'll be moving back to the Nashville area at the end of June.

Working for mostly small businesses has given me the opportunity to work on a wide variety of projects, and I have found myself tasked with maintaining existing websites and online stores fairly often. Keeping pages up to date with current information meant I spent a lot of time looking through existing code, searching online for short tutorials, and a lot of trial and error. I recently decided it was time to take the leap and start seriously working on my coding knowledge and skills. Looking forward to expanding my existing knowledge and learning some new skills.
